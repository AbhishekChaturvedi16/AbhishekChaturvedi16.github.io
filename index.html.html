// Sales Philosophy
The D.U.R.P. Framework

A consultative selling methodology built on empathy-first priming — understanding the customer before offering the solution. No cold probing. No pressure tactics. Just structured care.

01

Diagnose

Read the room. Observe body language, energy level, and unspoken intent. Prime the interaction with warmth and genuine curiosity — not interrogation scripts.

02

Understand

Uncover the real goal. Weight loss? Stress relief? Injury comeback? Athletic performance? Listen for the "why behind the why" — the emotional driver that makes someone walk into a studio.

03

Recommend

Map customer needs to OTF's science-backed offering. Explain heart-rate zones, EPOC, and Splat Points with data-driven confidence that comes from living the science, not memorizing a script.

04

Personalize

Craft a membership pitch tailored to their life — schedule, fitness level, and goals. Help them visualize themselves succeeding. Make it their story, not a product pitch.
